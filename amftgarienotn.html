#001 - Refined Shard, Clear Carvings

Documented on: 31-1-325 30.30 (cycle of the twin suns, revolution of fire, turn 6, phase 325, beat 30, pulse 30.)

Artifact Description

The first ever decipherable stave we could collect from the Catastrophe. Unfortunately it is also heavily cut off so we deducted over 70% of the stave remains to history. And most remarkably, this is the only shard that inverted the frequency of light it received.

Historical Significance

There are signs of math since there was also a variation of Snell's Law carved into the stave as well. They used a general function to determine the trigonometric.

Materials and Dissection

After deciphering the text to the latin alphabet, some of the words are still incomprehensive, cut off or not. We could only find "carve convex distortions on this surface" and "send out a beacx" most likely referring to beacon. The shard had the transparency of glass but the carvings hinted traces of carbon and... bismuth?

Current Condition

Since the shattered form it has taken it has never developed a single crack again. What it was crushed by seemed to be a meteor heavier than Io.

Theories and Interpretations

Theorists and scholars shared a likely theory: using immense light intensity of their current sun and the reflective properties of glass, they used the law of reflection to bend surfaces to reflect into a comprehensive message. (Check #017 for reference)
